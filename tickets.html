Tickets

Once again we are offering the Loyalty Scheme tickets to our loyal followers as well as a three-stage instalment plan. If you have been to the festival three times or more a ticket will cost €165, if you've been once or twice a ticket will cost €185 and if you haven't been before it will cost €205.
